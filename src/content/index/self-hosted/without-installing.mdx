# Using SurrealDB without installing

If you want to test SurrealDB immediately, open [Surrealist](https://app.surrealdb.com/) in your browser and use the built-in Sandbox connection.

The Sandbox is ideal for quick experiments, but its data is not persistent. To keep your data while still avoiding local installation, click **Deploy to Cloud** in Surrealist and create a free [SurrealDB Cloud](/docs/build/deployment/surrealdb-cloud/what-is-surrealdb-cloud) instance.

After deploying, switch your connection from Sandbox to the new cloud instance and continue working with persistent data.

When you are ready to self-host, continue with [Installation](/docs/self-hosted/installation).
