# AWS Marketplace

You can run SurrealDB Cloud with **AWS Marketplace** so subscription charges and related usage appear on your existing AWS bill. This is useful when your organisation standardises on AWS procurement, reserved-instance style commitments, or consolidated invoicing across vendors.

## Subscribe and link billing

1. Open the SurrealDB Cloud listing in **AWS Marketplace** and choose **Subscribe** (or **Continue to subscribe**), then confirm the contract and pricing terms.
2. Complete AWS account and payment linkage as prompted. Charges for the Marketplace subscription are billed through **AWS**, not a separate card on surrealdb.com.
3. Follow the **set-up** or **configuration** steps shown after subscription to connect the Marketplace purchase to your SurrealDB Cloud organisation—typically by signing in to SurrealDB Cloud and authorising the link.

## Provision instances

Once billing is linked, create and manage instances from the **SurrealDB Cloud** dashboard or Surrealist as usual. Instance creation, regions, and capabilities follow the same flows as direct Cloud sign-up; the difference is **who invoices you** (AWS) and how renewals appear in the Marketplace console.

If you change or cancel the Marketplace subscription, follow AWS guidance and ensure your SurrealDB Cloud organisation remains on a valid plan so instances are not affected unexpectedly.
