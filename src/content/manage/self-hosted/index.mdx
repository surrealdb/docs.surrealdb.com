# Self-hosted management

This section covers the operational tasks involved in running SurrealDB on your own infrastructure. For initial installation, see the [installation guide](/docs/start/self-hosted/installation). For deployment architectures and container images, see [self-hosted deployment](/docs/build/deployment/self-hosted/overview).

- [Configuration](/docs/manage/self-hosted/configuration) — server startup options, environment variables, and storage engine selection.
- [Backups & recovery](/docs/manage/self-hosted/backups-and-recovery) — export and import commands, backup strategies, and disaster recovery.
- [Monitoring & observability](/docs/manage/self-hosted/monitoring-and-observability) — health endpoints, metrics, and tracing integration.
- [Upgrades & patching](/docs/manage/self-hosted/upgrades-and-patching) — version upgrades, the `surreal fix` migration tool, and compatibility notes.
