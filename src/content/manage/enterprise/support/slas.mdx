# SLAs

**Service level agreements (SLAs)** for SurrealDB Enterprise Edition are contractual commitments between your organisation and SurrealDB (or an authorised reseller). They define **uptime** or **availability** targets for covered services and **response times** when you open support cases.

Severity bands (for example **critical**, **high**, **medium**, **low**) map business impact to **initial response** and **ongoing update** expectations. Critical issues—production down or severe data risk—receive the shortest acknowledgement windows; lower severities align with longer targets.

Uptime guarantees often exclude **scheduled maintenance** you agree in advance, **customer-caused** outages, and **third-party** failures outside SurrealDB’s control; read your order form for carve-outs and measurement methodology.

SLAs are tied to your **support tier** and region; confirm the numbers in your signed agreement rather than relying on informal summaries. For what is included beyond SLAs (phone access, named contacts, escalation), see [Support tiers](/docs/manage/enterprise/support/support-tiers).
