# Contact & licensing

To discuss **SurrealDB Enterprise Edition**, visit [surrealdb.com](https://surrealdb.com) and the dedicated [Enterprise](https://surrealdb.com/enterprise) page, and use the contact or sales options published there. The team can answer questions about **licence models** (such as subscription term, deployment footprint, and support tier), **pricing**, and any security or procurement questionnaires your organisation requires.

For product context before you engage sales, review [What EE is](/docs/manage/enterprise/overview/what-ee-is) and the [Feature table](/docs/manage/enterprise/overview/feature-table). Technical readers may also want [Installation](/docs/manage/enterprise) and [Licensing & activation](/docs/manage/enterprise/licensing-and-activation) for how keys and activation work in practice.

Official announcements, release information, and links to documentation remain on [surrealdb.com](https://surrealdb.com) and this docs site—bookmark both for your internal stakeholders.
