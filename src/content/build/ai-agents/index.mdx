# Why SurrealDB for AI agents

AI agents need more than a chat transcript: they need durable memory, structured facts, and often relationships between entities. SurrealDB gives you a single engine for documents, graph edges, vector search, and live updates, allowing your agent stack to stay within a single database instead of sprawled across separate concerns.

What that means in practice is that you can store conversation state and retrieved chunks beside canonical records (users, tickets, products) in one place.
