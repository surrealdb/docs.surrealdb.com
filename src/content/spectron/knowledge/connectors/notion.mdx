# Notion connector

> **Not implemented.** There is no Notion OAuth flow or scheduled sync in the current binary.

**Workaround:** export pages to Markdown or PDF and use `POST /api/v1/{ctx}/documents` or `spectron documents upload`.

See [Connectors overview](/docs/spectron/knowledge/connectors/overview).
