# Google Drive connector

> **Not implemented.** Spectron does not read Google Drive folders automatically today.

**Workaround:** download files locally or from CI and upload:

```bash
spectron documents upload ./exports/policy.pdf
```

See [Connectors overview](/docs/spectron/knowledge/connectors/overview).
